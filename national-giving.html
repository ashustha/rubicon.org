National giving

With a rapidly growing footprint, we are able to bring valuable insights, experience, and support to charitable organizations at the national level.

More and more of today’s challenges are of such size and scope that they require coordinated, nationwide action with the broadest reach possible.

Whether it’s developing the next generation of business and technology leaders in partnership with Seizing Every Opportunity (SEO), providing support for returning veterans with USA Cares, or working with the Salvation Army to help people all over the country tackle a wide range of issues in their daily lives, Rubicon is proud to stand with many of our nation’s largest charitable and social organizations and support the work they do every day.
