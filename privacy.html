Privacy Policy

Rubicon Holdings, LLC ("Rubicon") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this privacy policy ("Policy").   • 1. SCOPE AND APPLICATION

    This Policy applies to persons anywhere in the United States who use our Services to schedule pickups of waste and recyclable materials by independent haulers identified by Rubicon ("Users”)" For purposes of this Policy, personal information includes your name, postal address, email address, telephone number and any other information that may be used to identify you. This Policy does not apply to: (i) information we collect from or about the third party, independent hauling companies that are (or will be) under contract with Rubicon (collectively, "Haulers"); (ii) information we collect offline or through any other means outside of the Services; or (iii) information collected by any third party, including any content that may link to or be accessible from through our Services. If you interact with the Services as both a User and a Hauler, the respective Policies apply to your different interactions.

  • 2. UPDATES TO THE POLICY

    We may update this Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes in the way we treat your personal information we will provide you notice through the Services or by some other means, such as email. Your continued use of the Services after such notice constitutes your consent to the changes. We encourage you to periodically review the Policy for the latest information on our privacy practices.

    1. INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE TO US We collect information from you when you directly provide it to us, for example when you register and create or modify your account, request certain services, contact customer support or provide feedback, or otherwise communicate with us.
    2. INFORMATION WE AUTOMATICALLY COLLECT When you use our Services, we may automatically collect information about you.
      • Location Information: When you use the Services to schedule a pickup, we may collect precise location data about the pickup location from your mobile device. To learn more about these technologies, please see the Section below regarding Cookies.
